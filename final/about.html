Made and crafted from island grown fruits and ingredients, Sunset Ciders has been bringing together cider lovers
from all of the islands since 1975. We always make sure to provide our customers with the highest possible quality
cider in the Big Island. Unfortunately, with the rise of COVID, business has taken a toll on both us and many other
small business across the Hawaiian Islands. To hep keep our business afloat, we, along with many other shops in the
area, have been making rounds in local farmers' markets, helping each other provide enough income to save our
jobs and provide for our local families. Sunset Ciders will continue to support our fellow businesses in these trying
times as well as continue to provide everyone with tasty homemade cider made here on the Big Island.
